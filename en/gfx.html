Beyond additives — fuel & system optimization

Derived from native Australian hardwood extracts, GFX improves atomization, disperses sludge/asphaltenes, inhibits microbial growth, enhances lubricity, and stabilizes cold-flow performance.

Combustion Optimization

Non-ionic surfactants lower surface tension; polyol micro-explosions promote flame propagation for more uniform combustion and higher energy conversion.

System Cleaning & Protection

Disperses gums and wax into nano-scale particles, suppresses biofilms (“fuel bugs”), reduces injector fouling and carbon build-up, and protects valves/pumps.

Cold-Flow & Lubricity

Disrupts wax crystal formation and reduces viscosity for reliable starts in cold regions while plant-based lubricants reduce component wear.

Proven in lab & sea trials

  • Intertek (MGO): Higher energy density, improved lubricity & low-temp flow; carbon reduction up to 35%.
  • On-vessel testing: CO and NOx reduced by ~26%; exhaust temperature −2–3 °C.
  • Engine efficiency: Fuel consumption decreased by ~1.3–1.5% with improved hydrogen/oxygen content balance.

Results vary with fuel quality and engine conditions; many vessels observe even better savings in practice.

Simple dosing — rapid adoption

Initial treatment

Add 1 L per 2,000 L of fuel (1:2000). Use immediately after refueling.

Maintenance

Add 1 L per 4,000 L of fuel (1:4000) monthly.

Initial treatment

Inject 1 L per 5,000 L via dip port (1:5000). Allow ~48 h for natural mixing.

Maintenance

Inject 1 L per 10,000 L (1:10000) after a month or each full voyage.

Always follow onboard HSSE procedures and OEM guidance.
