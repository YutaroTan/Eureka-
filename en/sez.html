Croatia-Investment and Development

Special Economic Zone D.O.O. (SEZ), headquartered in Croatia, is committed to optimizing the local industrial structure and building the "Eurasian Green Industrial Park," providing comprehensive industrial support services.

Our mission is to establish a green industrial corridor connecting Europe and Asia, helping high-quality enterprises access the EU market and achieve strategic deployment and international expansion.

Through our efforts, we aim to foster green industrial development, promote regional economic growth, and realize sustainable development.
