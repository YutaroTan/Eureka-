About W.Y. Group

Dedicated to delivering intelligent and sustainable solutions through technological innovation and resource integration

W.Y. Group is a multinational industrial conglomerate with the mission of “Connecting global resources, driving industrial innovation.” Headquartered in the international business hub of Dubai, the Group leverages the Belt and Road Initiative to establish a global operational network spanning Asia, Europe, and the Middle East. The founder brings over 30 years of experience in large state-owned enterprise management, the maritime industry chain, and bulk commodity trade. Drawing on deep expertise from Hong Kong, Singapore, and Germany, the Group promotes cross-border resource synergy and cross-sector industrial integration to empower innovation-driven enterprise development.

W.Y. Group is headquartered in Dubai, with subsidiaries including: W.Y. (Europe) Holdings GmbH., W.Y. (Singapore) Holdings Pte. Ltd., W.Y. (Singapore) Pte. Ltd., W.Y. (Croatia) D.o.o.., and W.Y. (Hong Kong) Ltd.
