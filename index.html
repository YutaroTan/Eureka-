About Eureka (Germany) GmbH

Build a digital ecosystem for ship & shoreside services, enabling the digital and green transition of ports, shipping, and energy.

Eureka (Germany) GmbH is a cross-border industrial services company based in Hamburg. Working with industry alliances and partners across Europe and Asia—including a China maritime alliance (270+ members), Huayue Group, Eureka Group, Yingtai Shipping, and SEZ Croatia—we integrate technology, capital, and market resources to provide end-to-end maritime and industrial services.

Full Ship Industry Chain Services

In collaboration with the China Maritime Alliance (270+ corporate and individual members), Seawave Marine, and Eureka Germany, we are building a full-lifecycle service system covering shipbuilding and repair, maritime logistics, bulk commodity trade, maritime finance, and related services. Empowered by technology, capital, and market access, we deliver integrated, end-to-end global solutions.

Global Industrial Investment & Operations

Croatian Strategic Hub: As a strategic partner of the Eureka Group, we lead the development of industrial parks in Croatia, offering a one-stop solution that integrates investment in manufacturing, overseas warehousing, and supply chain finance-positioning the region as a model for industrial relocation in Central and Eastern Europe.

Digital Ecosystem & Industrial Empowerment
